Notes on fuel ratios:
######################################################################################

Given a fuel ratio 

3.81 / 1

To find the percentage of volume for each pressure vessel:


Add both sides of the ratio. 

3.81 + 1 = 4.81

Divide then divide each side by the sum. 

3.81 / 4.81 = 0.79209979209979

1 / 4.81 = 0.20790020790021

The resulting percentages can then be used to divide the space of the
pressure vessel envelope.
